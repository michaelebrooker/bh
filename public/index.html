WELCOME to Buhooro: a tranquil home on the edge of Lake Victoria, Uganda with its own private beach, and only 20 minutes from Entebbe International Airport.

The accommodation is a charming bungalow with views looking out to Lake Victoria set in about two acres.

Our private beach on the Lake is beautiful oasis of calm with many species of spectacular birds, including fish eagles, Egyptian Geese, and many species of Ibis, pied Kingfishers, and the iconic bird on the Uganda flag: The Crested Crane.

And near the house there can be seen spectacular pairs Blue Turocos and numerous other brightly-coloured birds such as Fire Finches and golden Weaver Birds that make their nests in the palms next to the house. Don't forget to bring binoculars!
